A Sandwich Soliloquy

O __________, creamy as the dawn,
Thou spread’st upon the bread with velvet might.
Thy noble thickness, smooth as polished fawn,
Doth promise comfort with each tender bite.

But lo! What sweetness through yon __________ breaks?
It is the apple’s glow—fair Honeycrisp!
Her golden notes, like autumn’s orchard wakes,
Do lend a crunch both delicate and brisk.

__________ jelly’s gleam, with nectar’s kiss,
By cunning hand is set in gentle place.
Yet Honeycrisp, with ruby edge of bliss,
Doth crown the feast—perfection, slice by slice.

Together, three in harmony unite:
The creamy, sweet, the crisp of orchard air.
In every bite, a symphony of light—
A sandwich fit for gods beyond compare.

O PB, apple jelly, __________ divine—
I pledge my heart. Forever thou art mine.
